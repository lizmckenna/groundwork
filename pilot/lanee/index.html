How to use it

Three minutes, then you're going.

The flow

  1. Two big cards at the top are your scoreboard. 5/26 sign-ups and 1-1s booked. Those are the only numbers that matter right now.
  2. Outreach grid = today's attempts. Empty squares are attempts you haven't made yet. Daily goal: 50. Each call/text/email fills a square. The color of the square tracks the outcome.
  3. Pick from "Up next." Five hot prospects at a time. They've either attended something before or been recruited by someone in your network — these are warmer leads.
  4. Phone numbers and emails are clickable — tap them on your laptop to dial via your phone (FaceTime/Phone Link) or open Messages/Mail.
  5. Log outcomes with checkboxes. You can check multiple. The grid square fills with the strongest outcome (green > dark purple > light purple > grey).
    · Called / Texted / Emailed — how you reached out. Multiple = a sequence (e.g. called, no answer, then texted)
    · Connected — you actually had a conversation
    · Signed up — they committed to the 5/26 Orientation
    · 1-1 booked — you scheduled a 1-1 with them
  6. If you check Connected, Signed up, or 1-1 booked, a next-step prompt appears. Pick from the dropdown or type your own. Required when there's a conversation — this is the guard rail.
  7. Hit "Save." The row greys out, the grid fills, the metrics tick up.
  8. Made a mistake or someone called you back? Click a greyed-out row to reopen it.
  9. Flag counter at the bottom goes red if you connected but didn't log a next step. Fix those before new outreach.
  10. When all 5 are done, click "Next 5 →" for the next batch. The button enables once everyone in this batch has a save.
  11. "← Previous 5" appears once you've done a batch, so you can go back and adjust if needed.
  12. 5/26 confirmation calls — once people start signing up, the "Confirm 5/26" tab fills up. Work that list 1-2 days before the event.
