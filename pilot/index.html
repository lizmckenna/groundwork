How to use it

Three minutes, then you're going.

The flow

  1. Look at the two big cards. They're your scoreboard for the week. 5/26 sign-ups and 1-1s booked are the only numbers that matter.
  2. Scan the outreach grid. Empty squares are attempts you haven't made yet. Each filled square is one call/text/email logged. The colors track what happened.
  3. Pick from "Up next." Five names at a time, with their phone and email. Don't agonize about order — they're already sorted by priority.
  4. Call, text, or email. When you finish, click one of the buttons on the row:
    · Attempted — left a voicemail / no answer / wrong number
    · Connected — had a real conversation
    · Signed up — committed to 5/26 Orientation
    · 1-1 booked — scheduled a 1-1
  5. When you click "Connected" or "Signed up," a prompt asks you to log a next step (e.g., "follow up to confirm 5/26 attendance"). Don't skip this — it's how nothing falls through.
  6. Your queue refreshes with the next 5. Keep going.
  7. End of week, check the Stats tab to see your trend lines and the ladder shape.
