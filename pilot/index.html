How to use it

20-minute walkthrough

The everyday flow

  1. Open Unattempted. Pick three names. Don't agonize — these are people who already raised their hand.
  2. Call or text. Use your phone. When you're done with each one, find them in Airtable and tap "+ Touches" on their record.
  3. Log what happened. Method (call/text), Result (no answer, voicemail, conversation, committed), one line of Notes if the call had texture. Takes 20 seconds.
  4. If you made an ask — separate from logging the call — open "+ Asks" on the same record. Type the ask. Mark outcome: Pending, Committed, Declined.
  5. Set the next step. One sentence in the Next Step field. Future-you will thank past-you.
  6. Move people on the ladder as you go. Talked to a Prospect who's coming to two more events? Bump them to Supporter. Talked to a Supporter who's recruiting friends? Bump them to Leader.
  7. End of week, open By Leader Ladder. Look at the shape. Where is the pipeline thin? Where are people stuck?
